NIKOLA

A smart energy monitoring system that helps operation managers lower operational costs for large buildings

OVERVIEW

Nikola is a complete energy and environmental monitoring platform that's suitable for both small and large buildings. Built using off-the-shelf and 3D printed components, Nikola is designed to be low-cost yet highly fuctional. Use the plug-level adapters to monitor the energy usage of any appliance! Also included are environmental sensors that can detect several environmental conditions. Supports mesh networking that operates seperately from your WI-FI network, so it won't interfere with normal web traffic. Each adapter also includes a remote switch allowing you to control if and when something should be on or off.

We are also developing unique algorthims to predict energy usage patterns and utility pricing so a time-based series of actionable options will be available to the user. This will allow complete control of how and when energy is used!

Designed and Engineered by Bob Gardner and Adam Jackrel

TECHNOLOGY

Built using custom hardware consisting of several XBee's and commercial power meters that have been hacked to allow for wireless communication. Using custom micro-controllers for each power node and a Raspberry Pi as the main server/master (collecting all the information.
